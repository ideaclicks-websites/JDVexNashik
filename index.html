Our Story

Engineering Excellence,
Rooted in Nashik

JDVex Industries was founded in the industrial heartland of Nashik — a city that builds. From our base at Pimpalnare MIDC, we have shaped the industrial corridors of Maharashtra one steel frame at a time.

Every beam we raise carries the weight of our commitment: structures that endure decades, not just inspections. We combine advanced fabrication technology with deep field expertise to deliver Pre-Engineered solutions that are faster, stronger, and more cost-effective than conventional construction.

Precision Engineering

Designed with advanced structural software. Built to IS & AISC standards.

Turnkey Delivery

Design, fabrication, supply & erection — one partner, zero coordination gaps.

Built to Last

High-tensile steel, premium coatings, and rigorous QC at every stage.

Core Services

From concept to commissioning, JDVex delivers industrial structures engineered for your specific load, span, and operational demands.

Pre-Engineered Buildings

Factory-fabricated, site-assembled steel buildings for warehouses, manufacturing plants, cold storage & logistics hubs. Up to 40% faster than RCC construction.

  • Clear spans up to 90 metres
  • Custom cladding & roofing systems
  • IS 800 & IS 875 compliant design
02

Industrial Sheds

Heavy-duty steel sheds built for machine shops, fabrication yards, storage facilities & MIDC industrial units. Designed for Maharashtra's wind & seismic zone requirements.

  • Light, medium & heavy-duty frames
  • EOT crane beam integration
  • Mezzanine floor systems
